Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our plans and billing.

Can I switch plans later? expand_more
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If you upgrade, the new features will be available immediately. If you downgrade, changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Is there a free trial for paid plans? expand_more
Absolutely! We offer a 14-day free trial for both the Standard and Professional plans. No credit card is required to start your trial, giving you full access to test out the features.
What happens if I exceed my user limit? expand_more
If you need to add more users than your current plan allows, you can easily add extra seats from your dashboard for an additional monthly fee, or upgrade to the next tier for better volume pricing.
Do you offer discounts for non-profits? expand_more
Yes, we support non-profit organizations with special discounted pricing. Please contact our sales team with proof of your non-profit status to apply for the discount.
