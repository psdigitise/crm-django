Data Deletion Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. If you have used Facebook Login to access our application, you may request the deletion of your account data at any time.

Requesting Data Deletion

To request that your data be deleted from our systems, please contact us at:

Email: info@erpnext.com
Subject: “Data Deletion Request”

Please include the email address or Facebook User ID associated with your account. Our team will delete all associated data within 48 hours and send a confirmation email.

Deleting App Access Through Facebook

You can also remove our app directly through Facebook:

  • Open Facebook → Settings & Privacy → Settings
  • Go to Permissions → Apps and Websites
  • Select our application
  • Click Remove and choose “Delete all data associated with this app”

Contact

If you need help or have questions, contact us at:

Email: info@erpnext.com
